# Bowline Design System

Bowline is a lightweight design system meant to accelerate the development of user interfaces with React. It is currently undergoing active development.

## Why?

There's no shortage of React component libraries and design-systems in the open source world to choose from. So why build something yourself?

Over the last several years, our team has spent considerable time working with clients to improve their websites and applications while also adapting to their current tech stack. That means for many of our projects, our technical choices are sometimes limited.
